Welcome

This project was made as a study of metroidvania game bosses, specifically in the game Hollow Knight. My aim was to answer the question:

What makes a boss fight "good"?

In the process of analyzing the various bosses of Hollow Knight, I came to the conclusion that there are 4 main components to a good fight. As a proof of concept, at the end I've included a boss of my own design to fit into the game that I believe embodies all 4 components.
